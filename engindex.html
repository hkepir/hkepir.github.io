Would You Like to Learn About Cahit Arf's Mathematical Legacy?

Get ready, because we're diving into the world of Cahit Arf :)

There's hardly anyone who hasn’t heard of this important mathematician. I first encountered his name during my elementary school years. While doing arithmetic operations with money in math class, I noticed the Arf Constant formula on the back of the 10 Turkish Lira banknote. However, I didn’t understand what it was for or where it was used. I came across this formula again during university. It turned out to be far more valuable than I had imagined—it was used in complex and abstract algebraic operations, but its applications weren’t limited to just algebra. My curiosity grew, and I began researching. I decided to transform my findings into a webpage and share them on my mathematics blog. For those who want to learn about the legacy Cahit Arf left for future generations, let’s get started.
